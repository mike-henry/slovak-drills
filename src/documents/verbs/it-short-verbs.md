# Verb Class: -iť (Short IT Verbs)

<section id="overview">

Short IT-verbs also end in **-iť** but use short vowels in some forms.

Examples:

- variť — to cook
- nosiť — to carry

</section>

<section id="stem">

Remove **-iť**.

- variť → var-

</section>

<section id="endings">

## Present tense endings

- ja: -ím
- ty: -íš
- on/ona/ono: -í
- my: -íme
- vy: -íte
- oni: -ia

</section>

<section id="notes">

Vowel length varies depending on stress and phonology.

</section>
