# Verb Class: -núť (NUT Verbs)

<section id="overview">

NUT-verbs often indicate a sudden action or change of state.

Examples:

- sadnúť — to sit down
- padnúť — to fall

</section>

<section id="stem">

Remove **-núť**.

- sadnúť → sadn-

</section>

<section id="endings">

## Present tense endings

- ja: -em
- ty: -eš
- on/ona/ono: -e
- my: -eme
- vy: -ete
- oni: -ú

</section>

<section id="notes">

Many NUT-verbs are perfective.

</section>
