# Instrumental Case  
Used to express **the means**, **the companion**, or **the tool** used to perform an action.  
Often used after verbs meaning *working with*, *going with*, *dealing with*, and with prepositions like **s/so, nad, pod, pred, za** (when static).

---

<section id="stems">

## 1. Stems

<section id="adjective-stems">

### Adjective stems
- Adjectives keep their base stem.
- Endings attach directly to the stem.
- Example: *dobr-* → *dobrým, dobrou*.

</section>

<section id="noun-stems">

### Noun stems
**Masculine**
- Consonant stems stay unchanged.
- Nouns in **-ec** drop *e*: *chlapec → chlapc-*.
- Nouns in **-a** drop *a*: *kolega → koleg-*.

**Feminine**
- **-a** → drop *a* (*žena → žen-*).
- **-ia** → drop *ia* (*kaviareň → kaviar-*).
- Consonant stems unchanged (*dlaň → dlaň-*).

**Neuter**
- Same rules as nominative stems.
- *mesto → mest-*.

**Exceptions**
- Masculine animate **-ec** and **-a** behave irregularly.
- Feminine consonant stems remain unchanged.

</section>

</section>

<section id="endings">

## 2. Endings

<section id="noun-endings">

<section id="noun-endings-singular">

### Noun endings — instrumental singular

**Masculine**
- Ending **-om**  
  Examples: *s chlapc**om***, *s muž**om***.

**Feminine**
- **-ou** for most feminine nouns  
  Examples: *so žen**ou***, *s kraj**inou***.

**Neuter**
- **-om**  
  Examples: *s mest**om***, *so srdc**om***.

</section>

<section id="noun-endings-plural">

### Noun endings — instrumental plural

**Masculine**
- **-mi**  
  Example: *s chlap**mi***.

**Feminine**
- **-ami**  
  Example: *so žen**ami***.

**Neuter**
- **-ami**  
  Example: *s mest**ami***.

**Notes**
- Neuters follow the feminine plural pattern.
- Masculine animate/inanimate share the same ending.

</section>

</section>

<section id="adjective-endings">

### Adjective endings — instrumental

**Singular**
- Masculine: **-ým** → *s dobr**ým** mužom*
- Feminine: **-ou** → *s dobr**ou** ženou*
- Neuter: **-ým** → *s dobr**ým** mestom*

**Plural**
- All genders: **-ými**  
  Example: *s dobr**ými** ľuďmi*

**Notes:**  
- Soft adjectives in **-í** follow the same pattern (*cudz**ím***, *cudz**ou***).

</section>

<section id="demonstrative-endings">

### Demonstrative endings — instrumental

**Singular**
- Masculine: *s t**ým***  
- Feminine: *s t**ou***  
- Neuter: *s t**ým***

**Plural**
- All genders: *s t**ými***.

</section>

<section id="pronouns">

### Pronouns — instrumental

**Singular**
- 1st: *so mnou*
- 2nd: *s tebou*
- 3rd M: *s ním*
- 3rd F: *s ňou*
- 3rd N: *s ním*

**Plural**
- 1st: *s nami*
- 2nd: *s vami*
- 3rd: *s nimi*

**Notes:**  
- *so* is used before certain consonant clusters.

</section>

<section id="verbs">

### Frequently used verbs with instrumental objects

These verbs often use the instrumental (usually with the preposition **s/so**):

- *pracovať s* — to work with  
  *Pracujem s počítačom.*
- *stretnúť sa s* — to meet with  
  *Stretol som sa s kamarátom.*
- *zaoberať sa* — to deal with  
  *Zaoberám sa projektom.*
- *manipulovať s* — to handle  
  *Manipulujem s nástrojmi.*
- *hýbať (niečím)* — to move (something)  
  *Hýbem rukami.*

**Notes:**  
- Instrumental expresses **companionship**, **tool**, or **method**.

</section>

<section id="prepositions">

### Prepositions governing the instrumental

The instrumental is required after these prepositions:

- **s / so** — with  
  *S kamarátom, so sestrou.*
- **pred** — in front of  
  *Pred domom.*
- **za** — behind  
  *Za školou.*
- **nad** — above  
  *Nad stolom.*
- **pod** — under  
  *Pod mostom.*
- **medzi** — among  
  *Medzi ľuďmi.*

**Notes / exceptions:**  
- These same prepositions use **accusative** when expressing **motion**.  
- Instrumental expresses **static location**, **accompaniment**, or **means**.

</section>
