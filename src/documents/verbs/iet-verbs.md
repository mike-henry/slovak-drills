# Verb Class: -ieť / -iet (IET Verbs)

<section id="overview">

IET-verbs end in **-ieť** or **-iet** and often show vowel alternations.

Examples:

- vidieť — to see
- vedieť — to know

</section>

<section id="stem">

Remove **-ieť**.

- vidieť → vid-
- vedieť → ved-

</section>

<section id="endings">

## Present tense endings

- ja: -iem
- ty: -ieš
- on/ona/ono: -ie
- my: -ieme
- vy: -iete
- oni: -ia

</section>

<section id="example">

## Example: vidieť

- vidím
- vidíš
- vidí
- vidíme
- vidíte
- vidia

</section>
